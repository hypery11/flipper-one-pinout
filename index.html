Flipper One Pinout

Interactive viewer for the GPIO header and the M.2 port. Click a pin to see its functions (and, on GPIO pins, the alternate-function mux configurations), or use the filter chips to highlight every pin that matches a given protocol.

Pins 12–19 are physically absent — that's where the Key-B notch sits on the connector.
